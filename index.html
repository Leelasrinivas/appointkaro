About AppointKaro

Founded by Leela Srinivas Atla, AppointKaro was born from a simple yet powerful observation: in the span of a 70-year life, we waste over 1 year and 3 months just waiting for appointments and services. This precious time could be spent with family, pursuing passions, or building dreams.

As CEO and Founder, Leela Srinivas Atla experienced this firsthand when routine appointments turned into hours of waiting. This frustration sparked a vision to revolutionize how India books appointments across 20+ service categories.

We've built a comprehensive platform that connects service providers and customers in a seamless digital ecosystem, eliminating inefficiencies in traditional appointment systems. Our location-based approach ensures that small businesses, which often cannot afford expensive SaaS solutions, can easily connect with their local customers.

Our mission is clear: give Indians back their most precious resource - time. We believe that every minute saved from unnecessary waiting is a minute that can be spent with loved ones, on personal growth, or simply living happily. AppointKaro isn't just about booking appointments; it's about reclaiming life.
