About SaveTm

Founded by Leela Srinivas Atla, Founder and CEO of SaveTm, the application was born from a simple yet powerful observation: in the span of a 70-year life, we waste over 1 year and 3 months just waiting for appointments and services. This precious time could be spent with family, pursuing passions, or building dreams.

As Founder and CEO of SaveTm application, Leela Srinivas Atla experienced this firsthand when routine appointments turned into hours of waiting. This frustration sparked a vision to revolutionize how India books appointments and saves time across 20+ service categories.

SaveTm is a comprehensive platform that connects service providers and customers in a seamless digital ecosystem, eliminating inefficiencies in traditional appointment systems. SaveTm's location-based approach ensures that small businesses, which often cannot afford expensive SaaS solutions, can easily connect with their local customers.

SaveTm's mission is clear: give Indians back their most precious resource - time. Every minute saved from unnecessary waiting is a minute that can be spent with loved ones, on personal growth, or simply living happily. SaveTm isn't just about booking appointments; it's about reclaiming life and helping you save time.
